Roadmap to Become a Doctor
  • Complete Class 12 with PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology).
  • Clear NEET entrance exam.
  • Pursue MBBS (5.5 years including internship).
  • Optional: Specialization via MD/MS.
  • Begin medical practice or higher studies.
Roadmap to Become a Lawyer
  • Complete Class 12 in any stream.
  • Clear CLAT or other law entrance exams.
  • Pursue BA LLB (5 years) or LLB (3 years after graduation).
  • Internships with law firms & courts.
  • Enroll with Bar Council & start practice.
Roadmap to Become an Engineer
  • Complete Class 12 with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Math).
  • Clear JEE Mains/Advanced or state-level exams.
  • Pursue B.Tech/B.E. in chosen field (4 years).
  • Internships & projects during studies.
  • Placement, M.Tech, or entrepreneurship options.
