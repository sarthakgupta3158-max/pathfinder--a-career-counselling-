Detailed Guide on Streams

Choosing between Science, Commerce, and Arts depends on your interests, skills, and long-term goals. Our guide walks you through every step with examples.

Roadmap: Science Stream
  • Choose subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Math, Biology, Computer Science.
  • Focus on analytical, logical, and problem-solving skills.
  • Prepare for competitive exams (JEE, NEET, etc.).
  • Careers: Engineering, Medicine, IT, Research, Data Science.
Roadmap: Commerce Stream
  • Choose subjects: Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, Math.
  • Develop skills in finance, management, and logical reasoning.
  • Prepare for exams like CA, CS, CMA, or management entrance tests.
  • Careers: Chartered Accountant, Banker, Economist, Entrepreneur.
Roadmap: Arts / Humanities Stream
  • Choose subjects: History, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Literature.
  • Focus on critical thinking, creativity, and communication.
  • Prepare for UPSC, journalism, law, or design fields.
  • Careers: Lawyer, Journalist, Designer, Civil Services, Psychologist.
Roadmap: Vocational Courses
  • Choose job-oriented courses like IT, Hospitality, Fashion, Agriculture.
  • Focus on skill-building and practical training.
  • Consider short diplomas or certifications.
  • Careers: Technician, Graphic Designer, Chef, IT Support, Skilled Trades.
